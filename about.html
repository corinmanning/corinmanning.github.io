About Me

Hi everyone! Thanks for visiting my website! My name is Corin Manning and here's my story!

On February 10, 2016, I tore my ACL and meniscus in my right knee. With only four minutes left in the last game of the season, I thought my sports career was over. I'm only in high school, but I have been a three sport student athlete. I injured myself playing basketball, but I also run track and play volleyball. On March 16, 2016, I had my first surgery and that's when the journey of getting back to my definition of normal began. The feeling of moving forward didn't last long because three months later, I had to have another procedure.

One reason why I constructed this website is for the MYP (Middle Years Programme) Personal Project. The Personal Project is your project to showcase the skills you have developed in your free time. This project is an individual project completed in your 10th grade year. It consists of three main components: a project, a process journal, and a report. You have the opportunity to choose your own topic and enjoy learning about something that you're passionate about while creating a final product. I chose to complete a project because it will prepare me for the Senior Project that is required in our senior year.

Another reason why I wanted to make this website is because I have noticed that not many people around me understand what an ACL tear is and how serious it is. As you can see, a lot of people tear their ACL's for various reasons. Basically, I thought that I would sit down and code this website to educate my friends and family, so they would know what I'm going through. An ACL tear is a very serious injury and could be career ending. I am so thankful to be a year into this and already able to run track again and will possibly be able to play basketball next season.
